Who Are We?


Hi, I'm Gillian Wayne, the lucky owner of this wonderful high end Bed & Breakfast. After discovering my connection to John Wayne, I visted the West of Ireland to see where the Quiet Man was filmed, fell in love with Galway and put down roots. I decided to open this Bed & Breakfast to ensure all visitors to Galway experience the magic I did when I arrived here 20 years ago.
